The Best Movies (according to some dude)

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Perks of Being a Wallflower

Incredible film. My #1. Haven't seen a better depiction of high-school life.

Interstellar

Actually broke my mind. What an incredible blend of epic sci-fi and a grounded, human story.

Dune (Parts 1&2)

Beautiful, mind-boggling, and intensely complex. Just a masterpiece.
